Contacts And Details of Our Company

West Coast Protection Agency now forms an integral part of GardaWorld’s African network.West Coast Security Company maintains a reputation of integrity, diligence and professionalism that remains unmatched across Africa. For over 50 years, we have delivered tailored security solutions to clients using our expertly trained and skilled staff.
